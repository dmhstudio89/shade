Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the iOS app Shade and its Safari web extension handle your information.

1. Information Collection

Shade does not collect any personally identifiable information. The app operates entirely on your device and does not store or transmit any personal data to our servers.

2. Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. This helps us improve app performance and user experience. The data collected includes general usage statistics (e.g., feature engagement), but it does not include any personal information.

3. Local Storage

Shade stores user preferences (such as dark mode settings) locally on your device. No data is synced to the cloud or shared with third parties.

4. In-App Purchases

Shade offers optional in-app purchases to unlock premium features. All transactions are handled securely through the Apple App Store. We do not have access to your billing or payment details.

5. Age Restrictions

Shade is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at your@email.com.
